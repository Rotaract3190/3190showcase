This projects main focus was to create awareness about farmers, farming and different type of farming like organic farming etc.
<p><img src="/assets/images/posts/riy18-19-iamfarmer3.png" width="100%" height="auto" alt="I am Farmer"></p>

We involved our social media handle to create awareness among people and rotaractors. We asked participants to follow different steps of organic farming and how one can easily do it in their household premises. 
<p><img src="/assets/images/posts/riy18-19-iamfarmer.jpg" width="100%" height="auto" alt="I am Farmer"></p>

Nearly 25-30 of them participated in the project and successfully completed the process of organic farming.

Our main aim to spread awareness was some what achieved and ever December month we will focus on creating and spreading awareness among society.
